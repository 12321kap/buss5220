Client problem

Inconsistent access

Hidden work stress

Product workflow

One product story: backend analysis logic, client-facing dashboard output.

01

Upload documents

Policy drafts, business-unit manager guides and grievance procedures are uploaded into the review workspace.

02

Clause-level scan

The system breaks long HR documents into clauses so risk can be traced back to exact source wording.

03

Risk diagnosis

Each clause is mapped against decent-work dimensions such as flexibility, stress, conditions and job security.

04

Fix Pack

HR receives revised wording, a manager checklist and worker-facing explanations for human review.

05

Audit trail

Every automated finding and human decision is retained for transparent governance and disclosure.
